For Teens

Access to an AI-powered bot to answer questions, write stories, and learn how to sift through results.

For Parents

Give your teens access to customizable bots, filter unwanted content, and access conversations with notifications.

Bots Available

  • Star Wars: R2D2 and C3PO
  • Harry Potter: Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger
  • Lord of the Rings: Samwise Gamgee and Gandalf

Subscription Options

  • Free: 4,000 words daily*
  • Basic ($1/month): Up to 400,000 words daily* to 20,000 words daily (higher cost models)
  • Pro ($5/month): Up to 2 million words daily* to 100,000 words daily (higher cost models)

* Estimates based on Nova Micro, a very low cost model from Amazon
